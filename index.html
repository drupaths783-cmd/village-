Welcome to Venmoney

A serene village nestled on the banks of the Achenkovil River in Chengannur, Kerala.

Explore the Village

Geography & Location

Located in the heart of Central Travancore, Venmoney (Venmony) is a picturesque village situated in the Chengannur taluk of Alappuzha district. It gracefully rests on the border of Pathanamthitta district.

9 km from Chengannur
8 km from Mavelikkara
River Achenkovil at South

Culture & Heritage

Sharngakavu Devi Temple

One of the most notable landmarks of Venmoney. Unique for holding no physical building structure for the deity in a sacred grove. The region is rich with ancient trees and local monkey populations, creating a truly spiritual atmosphere.

Vishu & Kettu Kazhcha

The village bursts into life during the Vishu festival. The spectacular 'Kettu Kazhcha', a display of massive, beautifully decorated chariots and stuffed wooden horses, reflects the grand artistic heritage of the people.

St. Mary's Orthodox Syrian Church

Dating back to 1017 AD, the 'Pazhaya Palli' stands as a profound testament to the ancient history and communal harmony of Venmoney village over the last millennium.

The Heart of Venmoney

Kaliyathara Hub

Known as the commercial and social heart of Venmoney, Kaliyathara is the central hub where the village converges. It serves as the primary junction connecting the community, bustling with daily life, local businesses, and social gatherings.

Unity & Harmony

Venmoney stands as a shining example of communal harmony and deep unity among its people. The coexistence and shared heritage of ancient landmarks like the Sharngakavu Devi Temple and St. Mary's Orthodox Syrian Church ('Pazhaya Palli') reflect a deeply rooted culture of mutual respect, where people cross religious lines to celebrate and live together.

Legacy of Great Personalities

Beyond its geography, Venmoney is renowned for its people. Supported by exceptional cultural and social conditions, many famous personalities, including esteemed scholars, artists, and leaders, have emerged from this village, carrying its legacy far and wide.

The Emerald Economy

Blessed Agriculture & Venmony Vettila

Blessed with highly fertilized soil and excellent agricultural conditions, farming forms the vibrant backbone of Venmoney's lifestyle. The village is endlessly surrounded by lush, high-yielding paddy fields, rubber plantations, and coconut groves.

Particularly, it is historically renowned for cultivating premium quality betel leaves, famously known as "Venmony Vettila". The superiority of the harvest here is so distinct that it was even recorded in the 19th-century Gundert Nighantu.
